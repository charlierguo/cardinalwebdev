F.A.Q.

When is the class taught?

The class is taught every Tuesday, from 7pm to 10pm. We won't always take up the entirety of that time with a lecture, but we will be around to answer questions afterwards.

Where does the class meet?

The class will meet in the study room in Arrillaga dining hall.

What is going to be covered?

You can look at the broad curriculum above, and we'll also be releasing a week-to-week schedule.

Are there pre-requisites?

We ask that you've taken at least CS106A, or the equivalent. Because of the short amount of time we have, we're going to focus more on web application specifics rather than general programming skills.

If you've done the JavaScript and Python courses on Codecademy, then you're in good shape.

Are all of the lectures mandatory?

They are all strongly recommended. You'll definitely get the best experience and skills by attending them all.
